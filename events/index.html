Live sports

Never miss the big game

Andy's is one of the best spots in town to catch the match. Screens run along the walls, so wherever you sit you've got a clear view — and a hot plate and a cold soda are only a wave away.

  • Premier League, La Liga & the Champions League nights
  • Cup finals, the derby and the big local fixtures
  • Come early on match day — the good booths fill up fast

The Andy's feeling

It's the room, and the people in it

Some of our favourite moments up here haven't been on the menu at all — a proposal by the fountain, a graduation lunch, a table of friends who came for chai and stayed till the second half.

Whatever you're marking, we'll make it feel like a big deal. Just give us a heads-up so we can look after you properly.
