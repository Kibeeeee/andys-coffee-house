Karibu Andy's

More than a coffee stop

Somewhere between your favourite café, a family restaurant and a sports lounge — right in the middle of town.

Coffee done right

Espresso, cappuccino, café latte, mocha and our own hot chocolate — from the machine, not a sachet. Single or double.

Real food, real portions

Full breakfast, pilau and biryani, kuku choma, nyama, sizzlers and family platters. Chai-to-dinner, we've got you.

Every game, big screen

Premier League, the derby, the finals — caught live on the screens with a cold soda or a milkshake in hand.

Room to settle in

Comfy leather booths, free Wi-Fi and a balcony over the street. Good for work, catch-ups and celebrations alike.

The room

Warm, lively and made to linger

Step up off Oginga Odinga Street and the town noise fades. Deep leather booths, striped timber tables, a gentle indoor water fountain and screens glowing along the walls — it's the kind of place you plan to visit for a quick chai and end up staying the afternoon.

  • Balcony seating that looks right over town
  • Free Wi-Fi & sockets — comfy for laptop mornings
  • Big screens for the match, plus space for the family
